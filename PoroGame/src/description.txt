placeholderName is a 2D side-scrolling adventure. The player plays as a little fuzzball tasked with destroying all the unicorns using
laser vision. The unicorns will jump around to avoid being hit by lasers. Unicorns that touch the player deduct a life from a starting pool of three.
Destroying unicorns awards points which will display on the win or lose screen. 

User Controls:
W,S,Space

W - Up
S - Down
Space - Shoot
